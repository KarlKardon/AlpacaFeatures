About Us

AlpacaFeatures is a social media organization that aims to provide entertaining alpaca content.

Not only do we provide entertaining content, but we put the money we make from it to a formidable cause. We donate 50% of our proceeds to credible animal rights organizations in order to give both power and purpose to our followers.

In order to diffuse any ambiguity of how our money is being used, we aim to disclose as much information we can as to how our money is being spent

For example, we share how much money is being made from our outlets of income including merchandize and advertising. We also show what organizations we are donating to and provide information on each.

Viewing AlpacaFeatures content is free and accessible, one does not need to pay a dime to support our mission to fight animal abuse.

The organization makes its income through advertising on its social media pages, selling merchandise, and receiving donations. Though donating and purchasing merchandise costs money, supporting the social media pages does not. When one interacts with our social media pages, they are increasing our value as a page to advertisers, making us receive more money from them overall.

Our Partners

Animal Equality

Animal Equality is an internationally based animal rights organization that works directly with governments and local organizations to fight for animal rights.

Best Friends Animal Society

Best Friends is an organization that is on a mission to make all animal shelters in the United States no-kill by 2025. In addition, they work to find all shelter animals a loving home.

Oceana

Oceana has a focused mission on reducing ocean pollution, saving marine wildlife, and educating on the dangers of pollution.
